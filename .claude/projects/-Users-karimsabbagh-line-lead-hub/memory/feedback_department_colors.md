---
name: Department color scheme
description: Fixed color assignments for each department - blue=sewing, purple=finishing, green=cutting, orange=storage
type: feedback
---

Department colors must always follow this scheme:
- **Sewing** = Blue (primary)
- **Finishing** = Purple/Violet
- **Cutting** = Green
- **Storage** = Orange

**Why:** These are the established brand colors for each department across the entire app. Consistency is critical for factory owners to quickly identify departments at a glance.

**How to apply:** Any time a chart, card, badge, icon, or visual element references a department, use the correct color. Never swap or mix department colors. This applies to icons, borders, gradients, chart lines/bars, and text colors.
